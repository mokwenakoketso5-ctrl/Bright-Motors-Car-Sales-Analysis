## **Bright Motors Car Sales Analysis**

### *Introduction*
The dataset for car sales has been provided, which captures daily transactional and pricing information for vehicles sold at Bright Motors. My duty is to analyse the data and extract actionable insights from the historical car sales and prepare a presentation that will help guide future sales and marketing strategies.

## *Objectives*
Determine;
- Which car makes and models generate the most revenue
- The relationship between price, mileage and year of manufacture
- Which regions/location have the highest sales volumes
- Trends in customer purchasing preferences
- Recommendations

## *Tools used*

*1. Miro - Planning and achitechture*
   - Demonstrated how the dataset will be handled from EDA to presentation
  
*2. Snowflake - Coding/ SQL Platforms* 
   - Handle missing and inconsistent values
   - Correct date column to timestamp
   - Calculated total revenue and profit margin
   - Used CASE statement to create categories
  
*3. Google sheets and Looker Studio - Data visualisation*
   - Revenue charts
   - Profit margin chart
   - Sales vs location charts
   - Sales trends
  
*4. Canva - Presentation / Report*
   - Reports and summaries
   - Recommendations
